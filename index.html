TCoAI Studio is a financial modelling tool for evaluating the Total Cost of AI Ownership across industries, business lines, and deployment scenarios. It accompanies the white paper Beyond the Hype: A Practical Guide to AI Adoption Across Industries.

How to use this tool
Model the Total Cost of AI Ownership for any use case across five industries. Configure your inputs across four tabs, run the calculation, and get a full 3-year cost and ROI analysis across three deployment scenarios.
1
Context tab — Set your organisation profile
Select your currency and region — this sets your FTE cost defaults. Choose your organisation size, then drill down to your industry sector, business line, and specific AI use case. A benchmark card will appear showing observed ROI for your sector from published research.
2
Deploy tab — Define your AI deployment
Select your AI type (Generative AI, Predictive AI, RPA, or Computer Vision), procurement approach (API, Buy, Build, or Open Source), and model tier. Enter your daily transaction volume and the number of FTEs currently performing the process AI will automate or augment. Use the pricing reference to compare token costs across providers.
3
Scenarios tab — Adjust deployment assumptions
Set the AI deflection rate — the percentage of daily volume AI will handle autonomously — and the FTE reduction rate for each of the three scenarios. Conservative (35%) is recommended for a first pilot. Base Case (55%) reflects typical production performance. Aggressive (75%) represents best-in-class deployment with strong data infrastructure.
4
Advanced tab — Override default assumptions (optional)
All cost assumptions are pre-populated with research-backed defaults. Leave them as-is for a benchmark model, or override with your organisation's specific figures — implementation cost, FTE annual cost, token pricing, indirect cost percentages, and ongoing cost percentages. Only edit what you know differs from the defaults.
5
Click Calculate TCoAI
Results appear instantly — KPI cards showing 3-year NPV, ROI, and payback period for each scenario. Five charts visualise your cost stack, breakeven trajectory, and token cost sensitivity. A year-by-year breakdown table shows every cost component. Switch between scenarios using the tabs in the breakdown table.
